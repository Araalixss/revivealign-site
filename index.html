Why You Wake Up Stiff

Side sleeping can leave your hips feeling stiff.

When sleeping on your side, the top leg often drops forward. That can twist the hips and pull the lower back out of alignment.

That pressure can build for 6-8 hours while you sleep. ReviveAlign supports your legs to help keep your hips in a more comfortable position overnight.
